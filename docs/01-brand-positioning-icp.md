# Brand Positioning, ICP, And Trust

## Brand Idea

Kuboqss is a premium Libyan beauty and self-care destination. It curates high-demand products for women who want visible improvement, simple routines, and a safe COD buying experience.

Arabic positioning line:

```text
كوبوكس - اختيارات جمال موثوقة للمرأة الليبية
```

Alternative:

```text
كوبوكس - منتجات مختارة بعناية لروتين جمال أوضح وأسهل
```

English internal positioning:

```text
Kuboqss is Libya's trusted premium beauty and self-care store for women who want practical, confidence-building products delivered cash on delivery.
```

## ICP

Primary ICP:

- Women in Libya, mostly 20-45.
- Active on TikTok, Snapchat, Facebook, and Instagram.
- Interested in beauty, hair, skin, body, personal care, confidence, and looking polished.
- Buys COD because it feels safer.
- Wants clear benefits, visible proof, and simple instructions.
- May distrust unknown online stores, exaggerated claims, and fake reviews.

Secondary ICP:

- Husbands, sisters, daughters, or friends buying a gift.
- Women in smaller cities who want access to products that feel modern and premium.

## Emotional Drivers

Use these emotions in copy:

- "I want to feel confident again."
- "I want something easy, not a complicated routine."
- "I do not want to waste money on another product that does nothing."
- "I want a store that answers and confirms fast."
- "I want to look cared for without going to clinics or salons all the time."
- "I want people to notice the difference."

## Trust Barriers In Libya

Likely objections:

- "Is this store real?"
- "Will they call me to confirm?"
- "Will the product arrive?"
- "Is it original/good quality?"
- "Does cash on delivery really mean I pay when it arrives?"
- "Will they spam my phone?"
- "Are the reviews real?"
- "Will the product work for someone like me?"

Website answers:

- Clear COD promise.
- Local Libya delivery wording.
- Phone confirmation expectation.
- Realistic benefit language.
- Reviews with city labels.
- UGC/video placeholders.
- Product usage instructions.
- Quality selection framework.
- Contact page with WhatsApp/phone/social links.
- Delivery and returns policy.

## Authority Framework

Kuboqss authority should come from curation, clarity, and proof.

Use these proof blocks:

- "مختار بعناية" curated by Kuboqss.
- "طريقة استعمال واضحة" simple usage instructions.
- "مناسب للروتين اليومي" designed for routine use.
- "دفع عند الاستلام" COD.
- "تأكيد الطلب قبل الشحن" order confirmation before shipping.
- "تجارب عميلات" customer experiences.
- "مكونات/مواد موضحة" ingredient/material explainer.
- "أسئلة شائعة بدون مبالغة" FAQ with direct answers.

## Tone Of Voice

Arabic style:

- Modern standard Arabic with soft Libyan-friendly wording.
- Avoid overly Gulf/Saudi dialect.
- Avoid Moroccan Darija on the site.
- Be warm, direct, feminine, and premium.
- Use clear Arabic CTAs.

Examples:

```text
اطلبي الآن والدفع عند الاستلام
```

```text
اختاري العرض المناسب لك، وسنتواصل معك لتأكيد الطلب.
```

```text
روتين بسيط، إحساس أوضح بالثقة، وتجربة شراء مريحة داخل ليبيا.
```

## Claims Policy

Do not promise guaranteed medical or permanent results.

Avoid:

- "يعالج نهائياً"
- "يوقف التساقط 100%"
- "يزيل التجاعيد فوراً"
- "نتائج مضمونة لكل الناس"

Use instead:

- "يساعد على دعم..."
- "مصمم ليكون جزءاً من روتين..."
- "اختيار مناسب لمن تبحث عن..."
- "مع الاستخدام المنتظم..."
- "نتائج وتجربة الاستخدام تختلف من شخص لآخر"

## Brand Visual Direction

Personality:

- Premium
- Calm
- Feminine
- Clean
- Confident
- Local but not cheap

Suggested palette:

- Deep plum: `#4B173D`
- Rose gold: `#C99A6B`
- Warm ivory: `#FFF8F1`
- Soft blush: `#F4DDE7`
- Charcoal text: `#211A1F`
- Muted taupe: `#8A747D`

Logo/header:

- Circle badge with `K` in rose gold or ivory on deep plum.
- Arabic text: `كوبوكس`
- English line below: `Kuboqss`

Font suggestions:

- Arabic: `IBM Plex Sans Arabic`, `Tajawal`, or `Cairo`.
- English: `Inter` or `Playfair Display` for premium accent.

Recommendation:

- Use `IBM Plex Sans Arabic` for body and UI.
- Use `Noto Kufi Arabic` or `Cairo` for headings if the logo needs stronger geometry.
